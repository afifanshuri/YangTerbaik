Cafe Section

All the best cafes in town!

Ali Cafe

Rumour says that this cafe tops all the other mahallah cafes in IIUM!. Packed with middle eastern foods, thousands of meals and not to forget their signature Mix Omelette! This cafe is located in the middle of the brother's Mahallah area but it is always visited by both sisters and brothers!

Bilal Cafe

What is Bilal Cafe without their famous Ayam Gepuk? Located just beside Mahallah Siddiq, this cafe serves more than just food, they serve happiness too! Come and visit Bilal Cafe, and you will be addicted! Guaranteed.

Ruqayyah Cafe

Although Mahallah Ruqayyah is far, but that does not stop students from stopping by at their cafe! It's strategic location just before the end of the road makes it's location easy to remember! So, you won't get lost if you're trying to grab one of their delicious afternoon meals again.

Sumayyah Cafe

Minimalist decoration of the cafe's interior makes eating dinner more appealing than ever. Not to mention, they serve ice cream! It is located beside the Convest Hill and just before Mahallah Nusaibah. One thing for sure, you will not regret spending your money here.

HS Cafe

What's more heaven than eating Cendol on a hot sunny afternoon? That's right, NOTHING! Never empty, always full. Just beside the riverside, it's not only favored by the humans, but also the lizards!

Evoke Cafe

Sweaty and tired after a long day of class? Make sure you reserve enough energy to climb the stairs to the ever refreshing Evoke Cafe! Other than equipped with air-conditioning, they also serve 1001 kind of foods. Eat here everyday in your life as an IIUM Student, you will still never get to taste all of their foods! Make sure you don't stop by HS Cafe because Evoke Cafe located just on top of them!

Edu Cafe

A few vendors only? But why there are no seats left? That's right, because the food in Edu Cafe is just as good as the others! Often visited for their famous Dim Sum, make sure you don't waste your chance to taste them too! Located near Bank Islam, you can cash out your money and spend it there at an instant!

Econs Cafe

Are you suffering from claustrophobic? Make sure to never set you foot to Econs Cafe! Why? Because it is always packed especially on lunch time! One thing for sure, you will regret it because their Ayam Gepuk is only second to Bilal's! So what are you waiting for? It's just near the Masjid!
